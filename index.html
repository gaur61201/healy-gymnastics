OUR PHILOSOPHY

More Than Gymnastics — We Build Champions

At Healy Gymnastics Academy, we believe every child has the potential to achieve greatness. Since 2013, we have been Kuwait's most trusted gymnastics institution, combining world-class coaching methodology with a nurturing, competitive environment.

Our FIG-certified coaches bring over 70 combined years of international experience, developing athletes who compete on the national and international stage — while building confidence, discipline, and character that lasts a lifetime.

📍 Located in the heart of Kuwait City, near Salmiya

OUR PROGRAMS

🌟

Toddler Gymnastics

Ages 3–5Beginner

Introduce your little one to movement, balance, and coordination through fun, age-appropriate gymnastics activities. Building the foundation for a lifetime of athleticism.

🤸

Recreational Gymnastics

Ages 5–12All Levels

Our most popular program. Children develop flexibility, strength, and gymnastics skills in a fun and supportive environment. Classes run 3x per week.

🏆

Competitive Team

Ages 6–16Advanced

For dedicated athletes ready to compete. Training includes floor, vault, bars, and beam. Athletes represent Kuwait in national and regional competitions.

💪

Boys Artistic Gymnastics

Ages 5–16All Levels

Specialised training for boys covering floor exercise, pommel horse, rings, vault, parallel bars, and horizontal bar. Building strength and precision.

🎀

Rhythmic Gymnastics

Ages 5–14All Levels

Combining gymnastics with dance and apparatus work (ribbon, hoop, ball, clubs). Develops elegance, coordination, and artistic expression.

⭐

Adult Fitness Gymnastics

Ages 16+All Levels

Gymnastics-based fitness for adults. Improve flexibility, core strength, and body control. Evening classes available. No prior experience required.

⭐ SIGNATURE EVENT

HEALY ANNUAL COMPETITION

Every year, Healy Gymnastics Academy hosts Kuwait's most prestigious gymnastics competition. Athletes from across the region compete in artistic and rhythmic gymnastics, watched by hundreds of families and judges. It is more than a competition — it is a celebration of excellence.
